# Warehouse Inbound Volume Dashboard

## Business Context
This dashboard analyzes inbound shipment volumes for a European warehouse
to support operational planning and risk monitoring.

## Key Business Questions
- Is inbound volume stable month over month?
- Which products and clients drive volume concentration?
- Are there abnormal weekly fluctuations impacting capacity planning?

## Key Metrics
- Total inbound boxes
- MoM and WoW volume change
- Volume split by product, transport mode, and client

## Key Insights
- Total inbound volume decreased by 14% MoM
- One product accounts for 46% of total volume, indicating concentration risk
- Significant weekly volatility detected around W46

## Business Impact
- Improve capacity planning and labor allocation
- Identify over-dependency on specific products or clients
- Support operational risk detection
